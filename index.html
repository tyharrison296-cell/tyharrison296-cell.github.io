Chain of Command

Board of Directors – Directors oversee the entire server and make voted decisions. Do not DM or ping them; use tickets or staff chat.
Management Team – Handle large reports, monitor performance, and oversee IA and lower staff.
Internal Affairs Team – Handle tickets, strikes, and staff infractions. Go to them before upper management.
Administrative Team – Oversee moderators, manage bans, and resolve serious in-game issues.
Moderation Team – Issue warnings and kicks, and ensure fair rule enforcement in-game.

Staff Regulation and Conduct

  1. Staff are not permitted to perform law enforcement duties while on duty. This includes speeding, running red lights, and running stop signs.
  2. Roleplaying of any sort while on duty is prohibited and will result in punishment.
  3. If a user asks to heal, always check for roleplay context first, to avoid ruining roleplays.
  4. Rank Uniforms: Staff members must wear their designated rank uniforms while on duty to ensure they are easily identifiable.
  5. If you are wearing the staff uniform, nothing should be covering it, so that community members can identify your rank.
  6. Staff Cars Only: Use only the designated staff cars while on duty. Personal or roleplay vehicles are not allowed for staff use during shifts.
  7. Professionalism: Maintain a professional and respectful demeanor with players and fellow staff members at all times.
  8. Impartiality: Enforce rules fairly and without bias. Treat all players equally, regardless of their rank or status on the server.
  9. You must use 4+ letters at all times for all commands.
  10. Chain of Command: Follow the chain of command by pinging the lowest rank online that can assist you. (Mod → Admin → IA → Mgmt → BoD)
  11. Communication: Keep open lines of communication with other staff members. Report any serious issues or incidents promptly.
  12. In any mod and all mod calls, the user MUST have video evidence. We do NOT take witnesses or logs as proof.
  13. Avoid arguing with members; you have the final say, and incidents can be reported through a ticket.
  14. If a user asks for a supervisor, finish the scene and guide them to open a ticket in our communication server.
  15. You may NOT ping or TP to HRs without permission. PM them instead.
  16. If you’re unresponsive for 5+ minutes in-game, it will be considered shift grinding.
  17. GTA driving includes recklessly driving in opposite lanes, crashing into poles, or driving medians.
  18. Do not park at medians or islands. Continuous patrolling ensures good RP.

Staff Outposts

Outposts are numbered 1 (most important) → 4 (least important). Always be performing checks while at an outpost and limit to 2 staff on duty per outpost.

  • Outpost 1 — Spawn: Highest priority; supervise spawn and expect VDM.
  • Outpost 2 — Gun Store: High RDM risk; monitor closely.
  • Outpost 3 — Mod Shop: Supervise intersection, FD station, and bank.
  • Outpost 4 — Three Guys: Calmer area; supervise city center and jewelry stores.

Guide

  1. Supervise River City Civilian Spawn, and surrounding areas.
  2. Supervise Gun Store, Fire Department, and surrounding areas.
  3. Supervise Mod Shop, the intersections nearby, the Bank, the Fire department, and surrounding areas.
  4. Supervise the City Center, the City itself, the Shopping Plaza, and Three Guys.

What you shouldn’t do

  1. NEVER just stand still; you should either be conducting checks or handling rule-breaking near your outpost.
  2. NEVER interfere with roleplay. Remain at your outpost unless you witness rule-breaking.
  3. NEVER help with police chases. Lay low unless there is clear rule-breaking.
